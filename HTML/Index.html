Vanilla Cake Recipe

This is the nicest vanilla cake I've ever eaten, thanks to its delicious vanilla flavor, soft, pillowy crumb, and rich, vanilla buttercream. And I promise you'll agree after just one bite.

Ingredients

  • Cake Flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking Powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Use room temperature ingredients
  3. Use baking paper in your pans
  4. Cool cake layers completely
